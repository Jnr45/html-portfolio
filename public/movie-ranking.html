The Best Movies According to JNR

My top 3 movies of all-time.

FAST AND FURIOUS 1- 8

High speed, crime and action packed

The biggest aim of this movie is to show the power of family, from the people you meet in the street, friends who become enemies and enemies who become your best friends.
The movie is also an inside peek in to underground street car culture and the various elements that make part of the culture

GREASE

1978 musical that shows the power of love

American Gangster

Based on the life stories on the worlds first Black mafia god father, Frank Lucas.
The movie takes us from the year where a mentor to mr.Lucas who always taught him to always look after the people in his community, is murdered in front of him after an annual charity drive, giving turkeys back to the community.
This all happens during the war in vietnam, he goes into the drug trade which during this time is controlled by the Italian Mafia, they will not allow for him to take part in the trade as they believed no black man can lead.
With the help of various family members he utilizes each ones background and strengths strategically to build an empire that not even the mafia has been able to build.
